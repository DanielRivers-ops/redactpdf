Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why can't I open or load a PDF file?

Please make sure your PDF file is not corrupted and that you are using the latest version of RedactPDF. Try reopening the app and importing the file again.

Does RedactPDF upload my files?

No. All PDF processing is done locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Is my data secure?

Yes. RedactPDF does not collect or store any personal data. Your documents remain on your device at all times.

Can I recover redacted content?

Once a PDF is exported after redaction, the removed content cannot be recovered. Please review your document carefully before exporting.

Why is auto detection not finding my text?

Some PDFs are scanned images and do not contain selectable text. In this case, please use manual redaction mode.
