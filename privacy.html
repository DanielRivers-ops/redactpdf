Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to RedactPDF ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

RedactPDF does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Uploaded documents
  • PDF content

All document processing is performed locally on your device.

2. Document Processing

All PDF redaction and processing occurs entirely on your device.

  • Your files never leave your device
  • No documents are uploaded to any server
  • No cloud storage is used
  • No third-party services analyze your files

Your privacy is fully preserved.

3. Data Storage

RedactPDF does not store any user data on external servers.

Temporary files created during export or sharing are stored locally and deleted automatically after use.

4. Third-Party Services

RedactPDF does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

No external SDKs collect your data.

5. Sharing

When you choose to share a PDF, the file is shared only through the iOS system sharing interface.

We do not receive, access, or store any shared files.

6. Children’s Privacy

RedactPDF does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Since no data is collected, no children's data is stored.

7. Security

We use system-level security features provided by iOS to protect your files.

All processing is sandboxed and isolated within the app environment.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page.

Continued use of RedactPDF indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@redactpdf.app

10. Consent

By using RedactPDF, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
